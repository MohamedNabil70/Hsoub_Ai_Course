Matching any number format:

-?\d+(((\.|,|e)\d+)+)?

in a single line:
^-?\d+(((\.|,|e)\d+)+)?$

--------------------------------------------------
Matching phone numbers:

(\d\s)?\(?(\d{3})\)?(\d{1,7})?((-|\s)?\d+){0,2}

Or

1?[\s-]?\(?(\d{3})\)?[\s-]?\d{3}[\s-]?\d{4}
--------------------------------------------------
Matching emails:

^([A-Za-z\.0-9]+)(\+\w+)?@\w+(.\w+)+
